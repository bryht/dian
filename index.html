Why Dian?

Translate faster, learn deeper

Built for language learners and polyglots. Dian stays out of your way so you can focus on words that matter.

🔍

Instant search & autocomplete

Start typing and Dian suggests words in real-time. Press Ctrl+F to jump straight to the search bar.

🌐

Multi-language at once

Translate a word into all your configured languages simultaneously. No more tab-switching between dictionaries.

⌨️

Keyboard-first workflow

Navigate with J/K, minimize with Esc, and switch languages inline with /en, /zh, /fr.

📤

Export your word lists

Save and export vocabulary to CSV. Import into Quizlet, Anki, or any flashcard app for spaced repetition practice.

🔊

Text-to-speech pronunciation

Hear how words sound in any language with built-in Google TTS. Perfect your accent as you learn.

🎨

Clean, focused design

No clutter, no ads. Dian's interface disappears so your words take center stage. Customize detail links for every language.

How it works

Simple, fast, intentional

Two steps from setup to fluency. Configure your languages once, then translate everything.

Step 1

Configure your languages

Pick the languages you know and the ones you're learning. Set custom detail links for deeper lookups.

Step 2

Search & translate

Type a word or phrase — Dian translates it into every configured language and saves your history.

Keyboard shortcuts

Dian is built for speed. Master these hotkeys and translate at the speed of thought.

Esc

/en /zh /fr /de …

Shift+J

Shift+K
Scroll word list up
